## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q. I found an error in one of your articles. How do I report it?**
Please email us with the article title and a description of the issue. We take accuracy seriously and will review and correct errors promptly.

**Q. Can I republish or translate your content?**
Short quotes with a link back to the original article are welcome. Full republication or translation requires prior written permission. See our [Privacy Policy](/en/privacy-policy/) for more details.

**Q. Do you accept guest posts or contributor articles?**
We are currently considering opening up contributor submissions. If you're interested, please reach out via email with your topic ideas and writing background.

**Q. Do you accept sponsored content or advertising?**
We may consider sponsored content that aligns with our editorial values and is clearly disclosed to readers. Contact us to discuss.
