## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What's the real cost of web hosting?**
Introductory prices ($3-5/month) typically double or triple on renewal. Budget for $8-15/month after the first year. Add $10-15/year for a domain name.

**Q: Do I need managed WordPress hosting?**
Not necessarily. Shared hosting with 1-click WordPress install works fine for most sites. Managed WordPress (Cloudways, SiteGround) adds automatic updates, better caching, and expert support — worth it as your site grows.

**Q: Can I switch hosting providers later?**
Yes. Most hosts offer free migration tools or services. It's easier than you might think, but it's always better to choose well from the start.

**Q: How much traffic can shared hosting handle?**
Typically 10,000-50,000 monthly visitors before you need to upgrade. For most new sites, this is more than enough for the first 1-2 years.

**Q: Should I get the cheapest plan?**
Get the mid-tier plan. The cheapest plans often have tight resource limits that cause slowdowns. The $3-6/month range offers the best value.
