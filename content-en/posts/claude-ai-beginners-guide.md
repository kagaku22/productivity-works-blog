"I use ChatGPT, but what's different about Claude AI?" "Should I switch?" — These are questions more people are asking every day.

The short answer: Claude AI and ChatGPT have different strengths. It's not about which is better — it's about using each where it excels. This guide covers everything from getting started to practical use cases.

## What Is Claude AI? The Basics in 30 Seconds

Claude AI is a large language model developed by Anthropic, a company founded by former OpenAI researchers. Anthropic's mission centers on building safe, reliable AI.

**Claude AI Quick Specs (May 2026)**

| Feature | Claude AI | ChatGPT |
|---------|-----------|---------|
| Developer | Anthropic | OpenAI |
| Latest Models | Claude Opus 4.6 / Sonnet 4.6 | GPT-4o / o3 |
| Free Tier | Yes (Claude.ai) | Yes (ChatGPT) |
| Input Limit | ~200K tokens | ~128K tokens |
| Japanese Support | Yes | Yes |
| Image Recognition | Yes | Yes |
| File Upload | PDF, text, etc. | Yes |

The standout feature is the **context window size**. Claude AI can process about 200,000 tokens in a single conversation — roughly 150,000 words. This makes it the best choice for analyzing long documents, reports, and manuscripts.

## How to Get Started with Claude AI (5 Minutes)

### Step 1: Create an Account

1. Go to claude.ai
2. Click "Sign up"
3. Register with Google or email
4. Complete phone verification (SMS)

That's it — no credit card required for the free plan.

### Step 2: Send Your First Message

Type your question or instruction in the text box at the bottom and hit send. If you've used ChatGPT, the interface will feel immediately familiar.

### Step 3: Upload a File

Click the paperclip icon to upload PDFs, text files, or images. For example, "Summarize the key points of this contract in 5 bullet points" will return a concise summary in seconds.

## Claude AI vs ChatGPT: When to Use Each

Based on extensive use of both tools:

### Where Claude AI Excels

**1. Long Document Analysis**
The 200K token context window means you can upload 100+ page PDFs and get comprehensive analysis. Contract reviews, research paper summaries, and manual Q&A extraction are all strong use cases.

**2. Natural, Polished Writing**
Claude AI tends to produce text that's well-balanced — professional but not stiff. Business emails, blog posts, and proposals often need minimal editing.

**3. Logical Analysis and Reasoning**
Complex analytical tasks ("Suggest 3 approaches that satisfy conditions A and B while minimizing C") tend to get more structured, thorough responses.

**4. Code Generation and Review**
Programming support is excellent. Claude Code can understand entire codebases and suggest contextually appropriate modifications.

### Where ChatGPT Excels

- Real-time web search integration
- Image generation (DALL-E)
- Plugin and GPTs ecosystem
- Voice conversation mode

### The Verdict: Use Both

| Task | Best Choice |
|------|-------------|
| Long PDF analysis | Claude AI |
| Business email drafting | Claude AI |
| Logical analysis & comparison | Claude AI |
| Web search-based research | ChatGPT |
| Image generation | ChatGPT |
| Programming | Both (task-dependent) |
| Blog post drafting | Claude AI |
| Quick Q&A | Either |

## 7 Practical Ways to Use Claude AI

### 1. Meeting Notes Summarization

Paste transcript text from tools like Whisper or Otter.ai and instruct:

```
Organize these meeting notes into:
- Decisions made (bullet points)
- Action items (with owner and deadline)
- Open items for next meeting
```

The 200K token limit means even a 2-hour meeting transcript fits comfortably.

### 2. Contract and Terms Review

Upload a contract PDF and ask "Flag any clauses that are unfavorable to the buyer." Final legal decisions should involve a professional, but this dramatically reduces the risk of overlooking important details.

### 3. Business Email Drafting

```
Write a business email with these parameters:
- Recipient: Department head at a partner company
- Purpose: Request a 1-week deadline extension
- Reason: Supply chain delay
- Tone: Professional and direct, not apologetic
```

### 4. Blog Post Outlining

Have Claude create the structure first, then write each section incrementally. For detailed techniques, see "[7 Tips for High-Quality AI Writing](/en/posts/ai-writing-tips/)."

### 5. Excel Formulas and Macros

Ask "Write an Excel formula to calculate month-over-month growth from sales data" and get a copy-paste-ready formula. VBA macro generation works well too.

### 6. English/Multilingual Writing

Describe your intent in your native language and get polished output in English — or summarize an English email in your preferred language. Claude AI handles multilingual tasks naturally.

### 7. Tax and Expense Categorization

For freelancers: "What expense category should this business lunch fall under?" Claude AI can suggest appropriate accounting categories, though final decisions should use proper accounting software.

## Prompt Tips for Claude AI

The quality of your prompts determines the quality of your results — regardless of which AI you use.

**1. Assign a role**
"You are a professional editor" or "You are a senior financial analyst" instantly sharpens the response.

**2. Specify the output format**
"In bullet points," "as a table," "in Markdown format" — explicit format instructions prevent ambiguity.

**3. Add constraints**
"Under 500 words," "avoid jargon," "lead with the conclusion" — constraints produce tighter output.

**4. Provide examples**
"Output in this format: (example)" lets Claude AI match the exact structure you need.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Is Claude AI available in languages other than English?**
Yes. Claude AI supports many languages including Japanese, French, German, Spanish, and more. Many users report its non-English output is particularly natural.

**Q: Can I keep using the free plan indefinitely?**
Yes, though message limits apply. Heavy users will benefit from the Pro plan at $20/month.

**Q: Is switching from ChatGPT easy?**
The interface is similar enough that there's no real learning curve. Think of it as adding another AI tool to your toolkit rather than switching.

**Q: Is it safe to input confidential work information?**
Anthropic states that user inputs are not used for model training on both free and paid plans (verify in their terms of service). For enterprise use, the API or Claude for Work tiers offer additional data protections.

**Q: Is there a mobile app?**
Yes. You can use claude.ai in mobile browsers, and dedicated iOS/Android apps are available.
